About Me

Professional summary

I work on low-level embedded software for automotive battery management systems, with hands-on experience in production-oriented firmware development on NXP S32K322 platforms. My recent work includes CDD development for ADI battery monitoring and pack monitoring ICs, data acquisition for battery parameters, battery monitoring, passive cell balancing using PWM-based control, functional safety-oriented diagnostics, and low-power BMS features.

I have worked on FS26-based power management and enabled Low Power Cell Monitoring to detect abnormal battery conditions in low-power mode and wake the controller when required. I use CANoe, CANalyzer, CAPL scripts, CAPL DLLs, Freemaster, logic analyzer, and log analysis tools extensively during integration, debugging, and validation activities. I also integrate application software generated from MBD models and have developed software for external EEPROM and external flash.
